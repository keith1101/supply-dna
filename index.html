Problem & Solution

Common Challenges

Lack of Transparency

Fragmented data and opaque supply chains hinder end-to-end visibility.

Counterfeit Components

Vulnerable to fraudulent parts, risking product integrity and brand reputation.

Inefficient Recalls

Slow, costly, and broad-scope recalls due to poor component tracing.

Supply DNA's Solution

End-to-End Visibility

Blockchain-powered immutable ledger for real-time, transparent tracking.

Authenticity Guaranteed

Securely verify component origins, preventing unauthorized substitutions.

Precision Recalls

Pinpoint affected units for surgical, cost-effective product recalls.

Core Technologies

Blockchain

Immutable ledger for secure, transparent data.

Ensures data integrity and prevents tampering across the supply chain, providing an unalterable record.

AIoT Sensors

Real-time environmental and location data capture.

Collects precise environmental data (temp, humidity, shock) and location during component transit and storage.

AI/ML

Data analysis, anomaly detection, and predictive insights.

Analyzes vast datasets to identify anomalies, predict potential failures, and optimize logistics.

QR/NFC Tagging

Unique identifiers linking physical components to digital twins.

Provides seamless, scannable links for immediate access to component lineage and data history.

Transformative Benefits

Before Supply DNA

  • Limited Visibility: Opaque and fragmented supply chains.

  • Counterfeit Risk: Vulnerability to fraudulent components.

  • Inefficient Recalls: Slow, broad, and costly product recalls.

  • ESG Compliance Burden: Difficulty in proving ethical sourcing.

  • Brand Reputation Risk: Damage from product failures or scandals.

After Supply DNA

  • Full Traceability: Component-level, immutable, real-time tracking.

  • Guaranteed Authenticity: Verified component origin and history.

  • Surgical Recalls: Precision targeting reduces costs and impact.

  • Simplified ESG: Transparent data for compliance and reporting.

  • Enhanced Trust: Builds strong brand loyalty and confidence.

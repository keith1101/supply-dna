What is Supply DNA?

Supply DNA is a decentralized protocol designed to eliminate ambiguity and fraud in complex supply chains. By creating a unique, immutable digital identity for every component and product using AI‑enabled IoT devices, we record its entire journey on a secure blockchain. This transparent, tamper‑proof “digital twin” can be verified at any point, ensuring authenticity and compliance from the deepest tiers of your network.

How It Works

1. Digital Tagging

IoT sensors (NFC/QR) assign a unique digital ID to raw materials at the source.

2. Blockchain Entry

The ID and material data are hashed and recorded as the first block in its unique chain.

3. AI‑Powered Scans

AI verifies data at each production stage. Every scan adds a new, immutable block.

4. Real‑time Tracking

Stakeholders can access the complete, verified history of any item instantly.

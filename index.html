Problem & Solution

Common Problems

Lack of Transparency

Difficult to track components across multi-tier supply chains, leading to blind spots.

Counterfeit Components

Risk of fraudulent or sub-standard components entering the supply chain.

Inefficient Recalls

Slow and costly product recalls due to lack of precise component lineage.

Supply DNA's Solution

End-to-End Visibility

Provides immutable, real-time tracking of every component from origin to final product.

Authenticity Guaranteed

Blockchain's immutability ensures all data points are tamper-proof and verifiable.

Precision Recalls

Pinpoint affected components, enabling highly targeted and efficient recalls.

Technologies Used

Blockchain

Immutable ledger for secure, transparent data.

Ensures data integrity and prevents tampering across the supply chain.

IoT Sensors

Real-time data capture from components.

Collects environmental data (temp, humidity) and location during transit.

AI/ML

Data analysis, anomaly detection, predictions.

Identifies anomalies, predicts potential issues, and optimizes supply chain routes.

QR/NFC Tagging

Unique identifiers for each component.

Provides a simple, scannable link between physical components and digital twins on the blockchain.

How It Works – Simplified Workflow

1

Component Tagged

Each component receives a unique QR/NFC tag.

2

Sensor Data Recorded

IoT sensors collect real-time data during transit.

3

Product Assembled

Components are assembled; a unique "DNA" is formed.

4

Data to Blockchain

Lineage data and sensor logs are written to the blockchain.

5

Product Scanned

Scan product to instantly retrieve its full lineage.
